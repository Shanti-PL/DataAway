Setting

How to Play

  • You must keep at least one heart and guide the NPC to their desired destination. You can make a choice at each session, but if the NPC doesn't like it, you will be deprived of a heart.
  • Don't be concerned! As the game progresses, try to select the NPC's prefered option. The game is over if all three hearts are lost.
  • You can try again the next time the game starts to understand the conversation with the NPC before making a decision.

Step 1:

Choose an NPC you like based on their characteristics for dating.

Step 2:

Click option buttons you want to trigger more information by the NPC

Step 3:

Enjoy the story, keep your heart, and guide NPCS to their desired destination.

Tips:

Take your time reading the story. The preferences of NPCs are hidden in the dialogue. Try to make decisions based on their preferences.
